2025 IEEE 4th International Conference on Technology, Engineering, Management for Societal Impact using Marketing, Entrepreneurship, and Talent (TEMSMET)

Conference Theme

“Technology Management and Innovation for Sustainable Development in the era of Gen AI”
This theme focuses on the intersection of technology management, innovation, and sustainable development within the context of the rapidly advancing era of Generative Artificial Intelligence (Gen AI).

Key Concepts

  • Generative AI (Gen AI): AI systems that can create new data, designs, or content mimicking human creativity.
  • Technology Management: Planning, development, and implementation of technological innovations to achieve strategic goals.
  • Innovation: Creating new ideas, products, or methods that deliver value and solve global challenges.
  • Sustainable Development: Meeting the needs of the present without compromising future generations.

Objectives

  • To showcase the latest innovations in AI and their applications in engineering and technology management.
  • To discuss the challenges and opportunities of integrating AI into traditional engineering and management practices.
  • To explore strategic management practices that leverage AI to enhance productivity and innovation.
  • To facilitate networking and collaboration among professionals, academics, and industry leaders.

Call for Papers

We invite submissions addressing innovative research, practical applications, case studies, or theoretical advancements in AI, technology, engineering, and management.

Submission Deadline:

July 06, 2025

Key Topics

  • AI in Engineering and Technology: Case Studies and Applications
  • Managing AI-Driven Engineering Projects: Challenges and Solutions
  • The Role of AI in Sustainable Engineering and Development
  • Integrating AI into Engineering Design and Manufacturing Processes
  • Ethical AI and Its Impact on Engineering and Technology Management
  • Data-Driven Decision Making in Technology Management

Conference Structure

  • Keynote Speeches by renowned experts
  • Panel Discussions on AI ethics and engineering education
  • Technical Sessions on cutting-edge research and case studies
  • Workshops for hands-on engagement with AI tools
  • Poster Sessions for researchers and students
  • Networking Events for professionals across sectors

Potential Speakers

  • Industry Leaders: CEOs, CTOs, and Innovation Officers
  • Academics and Researchers: Professors and scholars specializing in AI
  • Government Representatives: Policy makers in AI and technology regulation
  • Entrepreneurs: Founders of AI startups
